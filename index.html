About GATO Cycles

We specialize in creating custom, handbuilt bicycle frames tailored to your unique riding style and preferences. With years of experience in the craft, we combine traditional frame building techniques with modern materials and design principles.

Every frame we build is a collaboration between our expertise and your vision. From initial consultation through final delivery, we're committed to creating a bicycle that not only performs exceptionally but also reflects your personal style and riding goals.

Our Services

Custom Frame Building

Handcrafted bicycle frames built to your exact specifications and measurements. We work with steel, titanium, and carbon fiber to create your perfect ride.

Bicycle Repairs

Complete repair services for all types of bicycles, from basic tune-ups to complex overhauls. We service road, mountain, gravel, and vintage bicycles.

Consultation & Fitting

Expert advice on frame geometry, materials, and design. Professional bike fitting ensures optimal comfort and performance for your custom build.
